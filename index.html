This course was created for IT Sourcers and Recruiters:
for Juniors to explain the best sourcing strategies and for Senior Professionals to advance their skills.

Tech companies are going through a significant expansion towards a more digitalized world fueling increased demand for tech talent and of course Tech Sourcers and Recruiters. 65% of all technology leaders across 83 countries had claimed difficulties with tech hiring as one of the major challenges according to IT outsourcing service provider Harvey Nash's survey.

Currently, recruiting experienced tech talent professionals from various sources is a big advantage. GitHub sourcing course allows tapping into a rich talent pool and filling the skills gap by looking beyond LinkedIn.

GitHub IT Sourcing Course was created by Mariam Darsavelidze, IT Recruitment Team Lead from ManpowerGroup Talent Solutions - the world leader in innovative workforce solutions. Besides being a Recruiter, Mariam is passionate about Front-End and uses GitHub as a Web Developer. This experience gives her the advantage to explain in detail how GitHub works and how Recruiters can benefit from this online platform.

Currently, GitHub is where over 65 million developers build, host, collaborate on innovative projects, contribute to the open-source community, and shape the future of software together. That said, GitHub has to offer Technical Sources and Recruiters a long list of the best matching profiles with some unique candidates, who don't have accounts on LinkedIn or have deleted the information, because they were overwhelmed with the number of InMails.


Hack Tech Sourcing and Reach Out to Hidden Talents in 2022!

This course is focused on 4 search strategies that can be used for tech talent sourcing on GitHub:

Search Using Visual Interface

This guide will walk you through a variety of searching filters by users, repositories, files, issues, etc.

 1st strategy
Advanced Search Qualifiers

With the use of 26 search qualifiers, you will boost your sourcing and find candidates right from the search bar.

 2nd strategy
X-Ray Search

With the use of the X-Ray strategy and the collection of my strings, you will find any best matching profile or CV on GitHub.

 3rd strategy
CSE (Programmable search)

You will master GutHub's structured data search with CSE and be able to find people with the use of metatags.

 4th strategy

The course goal is to upskill you into a Professional Sourcer. This material will be extremely useful for those with no prior experience in Recruitment or Sourcing, as well as for those who want to strengthen their skills, and find the best matching candidates faster than ever!

The sourcing guide material is full of ready-to-use strings, search operators, explanations, hacks, and techniques that you will not find online!
